My interest in photography started at a young age (yes that's me above with my dad's light meter). My dad had always taken photos and bought me a Polaroid camera when I was around five years old. I love taking photos of sporting events and landscapes. I take photos for my college's newpaper, the Scarlet and Black, and have been published on Time.com here!
